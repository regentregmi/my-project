Nature of Guffapokhari, Tinjure, and Nundhaki

Guffapokhari is a serene and hidden lake situated in the Eastern Himalayas of Nepal. This pristine lake is surrounded by lush forests and the towering mountains of the region. Guffapokhari offers spectacular views of the Kanchenjunga range, one of the highest peaks in the world. The area is known for its unspoiled natural beauty, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and those looking for peace and tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of modern life.

Tinjure is a picturesque village located in the foothills of the eastern Himalayas, known for its vibrant rhododendron forests that bloom in spring, creating a magical scene of colors. The region is a rich biodiversity hotspot, home to a variety of flora and fauna, and is an excellent destination for birdwatching. The area also offers trekking trails, with panoramic views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and mountains.

**Nundhaki** is another beautiful and tranquil area in the eastern region of Nepal, nestled in the wilderness, offering a peaceful escape from city life. This area is blessed with rich biodiversity, including diverse wildlife, lush green forests, and clear rivers. Nundhaki is a perfect place for those who want to explore the pristine natural beauty of Nepal and immerse themselves in its vibrant flora and fauna. It’s a fantastic location for nature walks, wildlife photography, and trekking.
